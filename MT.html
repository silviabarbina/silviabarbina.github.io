Bibliography

No single textbook covers exactly the material we will cover with exactly the same notation and conventions. However, the textbooks below collectively provide a complete set of references, and go well beyond the contents of this course.

Freely available on the web:

  • D. Zambella, A Crèche Course in Model Theory. AMS Open Math Notes, 2018. (These notes are a working draft - the latest version is here.)

Available in print:

  • J. Kirby, An Invitation to Model Theory. CUP, 2019
  • D. Marker, Model Theory: an Introduction. Springer, 2002
  • K. Tent, M. Ziegler, A Course in Model Theory. Lecture Notes in Logic 40, CUP 2012
